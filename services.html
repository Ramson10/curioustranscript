Services

Whether you're a content creator, educator, or researcher, accurate transcription adds clarity, accessibility, and long-term value to your work.

As a skilled transcriber with a background in content writing, editing, and tech, I offer reliable, high-quality transcription services tailored to your needs.

Verbatim Transcription

Capture every word exactly as it was spoken - yes, including the "uhs", "ums", and stutters.

This style is perfect for legal records, interviews, qualitative research, and anything that demands a full, raw transcript.

Clean/Edited Transcription

Prefer your transcript polished and easier to read? I’ll remove filler words, false starts, and repetitions while preserving the speaker's meaning and tone.

Ideal for publishing, presentations, articles, or client-ready documents.

Multi-Speaker & Difficult Audio Transcription

Have a file with overlapping voices, strong accents, or background noise? No worries. I can handle challenging audio and clearly distinguish between multiple speakers for clarity and structure.

Proofread & Edited Transcripts

If you already have a transcript but need it professionally reviewed, I’ll clean up grammar, punctuation, formatting, and consistency to ensure a highly polished final document.
