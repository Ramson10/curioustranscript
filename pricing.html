Pricing

Rates are customized based on audio quality, number of speakers, and turnaround time. Here's a general overview:

Service Description Rate (per audio min)
Clean Verbatim (No Timestamps) Readable transcript without fillers or stutters $1.00
Clean Verbatim (With Timestamps) Readable transcript + timestamps at intervals $1.25
Full Verbatim (No Timestamps) All words and sounds included (e.g., "um", "uh") $1.50
Full Verbatim (With Timestamps) Full verbatim with time markers $2.00
Edited Transcripts Grammar and clarity enhancement of existing transcripts $0.60 or $30/hr
Speaker Labeling Speakers identified and labeled + $0.20
Difficult Audio Background noise, heavy accents, poor quality + $0.50
Rush Delivery Turnaround under 24 hours + 75% of base rate

Note that bulk or continous projects attract discount.
